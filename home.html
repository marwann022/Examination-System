Features

Everything You Need to Excel

ExamHub is built with powerful tools that create a realistic, fair, and stress-free environment for every student.

Auto-Save Progress

Your answers, notes, and timer are saved automatically every few seconds. Reload the page without fear — you'll return to exactly where you left off.

Live Countdown Timer

A prominent 30-minute timer counts down in real time. Visual urgency cues change color as time runs low, and the exam auto-submits when time expires.

Question Bookmarks

Flag any question for quick review later. Bookmarks are persisted alongside your answers so you never lose track of tricky questions mid-exam.

Per-Question Notes

Jot down reasoning or reminders for each individual question in a dedicated notes panel. Notes are saved along with your answers automatically.

Built-in Calculator

A floating mini-calculator is always a click away during the exam — no need to switch tabs for quick arithmetic, keeping your focus intact.

Detailed Results

After submission you get a thorough breakdown: every question, your answer vs the correct one, your score percentage, and a clear PASSED or FAILED verdict.
